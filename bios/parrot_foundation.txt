
Jonathan "Duke" Leto has a Masters degree in Mathematics from the University of
Central Florida and has several published papers in peer-reviewed journals in 
the fields of mathematics, engineering and bioinformatics.

Jonathan has been involved with Parrot since 1.0, and has been Organization
Administrator for Parrot Foundation in Google Summer of Code 2009-2010 and
Google Code-In 2010. He was elected as Secretary on the Board of Directors in
August 2010 and took the role of Community Manager in October 2010.

He currently works as a Bioinformatics Analyst at Boyce Thompson Institute for
Plant Research at Cornell University and enjoys discovering wheels within wheels.
